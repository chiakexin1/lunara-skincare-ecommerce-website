## 🌟 Project Overview
Despite the rapid growth of beauty e-commerce, modern consumers face persistent issues like product overload, counterfeit goods, and a lack of skincare personalization. 

**Lunara Skincare** bridges the gap between trust and convenience. It blends the strong ethical presence of brands like *Lush*, the structured product variety of *Sephora*, and the high accessibility of platforms like *Shopee*, while avoiding visual clutter and untrusted third-party vendors. It serves as a curated digital companion that guides users to secure, science-backed skincare solutions.

---

## ✨ Key Features
The website features 8+ cohesive, semantic HTML5 pages designed with an intuitive UI/UX flow:

*   **Homepage:** Prominently highlights curated brands, certified benefits (100% Authentic, Fast Delivery), best-sellers, and user testimonials.
*   **Product Catalog & Brand Filtering:** Full product list with customized brand-specific landing views (Skintific, La Roche-Posay, COSRX, Eucerin).
*   **Rich Product Details:** Displays detailed usage instructions, pricing, and precise key ingredients.
*   **State Management & Persistence:** Persistent registration, secure login validation (with password visibility toggle), and continuous shopping cart sessions via cookies and Local/Session Storage.
*   **Dynamic Cart & Checkout:** Real-time summary tracking, reactive subtotal updates, free-shipping threshold indicators (Free above RM80), and comprehensive multi-option checkouts (Credit Card, E-Wallet, Online Banking).
*   **Support & Contact Engine:** Responsive contact form validation, physical corporate address details, business hours, and an automated FAQ section.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Implementations
*   **Semantics & Layout:** HTML5 structural elements, CSS3 properties, and a fully fluid mobile-responsive responsive grid.
*   **Interactive Components:** JavaScript (ES6+) and jQuery plugins for high-performance visual transitions and real-time form validation.
*   **API & Plugins:** Integration of external RESTful APIs and interactive social media plugins.
*   **Client-Side Browser Storage:** 
    *   Cookies/Local Storage for persistent cross-session shopping carts.
    *   Session Storage for real-time order tracking statuses and temporary authentication records.
